UI/UX Design

UI/UX design is a field that focuses on creating a positive user experience for a product or service. The goal is to make the user's interaction with a product or service easy, efficient, and enjoyable. UI/UX designers work together to achieve this goal, with UI designers focusing on the look and feel of a product, and UX designers focusing on the user's overall experience.
